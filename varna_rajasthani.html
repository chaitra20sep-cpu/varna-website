Our Heritage

"In Jaipur, every grain of gold

VARNA was born in the pink lanes of Jaipur — where karigars have hammered gold and polished gemstones for the royals of Rajputana for five centuries. We carry that legacy into your everyday life. Because the finest jewelry shouldn't wait in a glass case. It belongs on you, marking every chapter of your story.
